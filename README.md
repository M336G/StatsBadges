# Stats Badges
Gives <cb>cool badges</c> to players depending on their <cb>in-game statistics</c>!

## Badges requirements
Each badge's requirements will be displayed below. Note that the demon badges are cumulated; there can only be one of them appearing.
- **<cy>Star collector</c>:** at least 3,000 stars.
- **<cl>Moon collector</c>:** at least 3,000 moons.
- **<cb>Creator</c>:** at least 1 creator point.
- **<co>Secret coin collector</c>:** at least 100 secret coins.
- **<cl>User coin collector</c>:** at least 1,000 user coins.
- **<cr>Demon slayer</c>:** beaten at least 100 demon levels.
- **<cr>Demon master</c>:** beaten at least 50 demon levels.
- **<cr>Demon chaser</c>:** beaten at least 25 demon levels.
